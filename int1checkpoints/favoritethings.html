Elephants!

Elephants evolved from mammoths and live in places like Africa, India, and Southeast Asia. They eat grasses,
small plants, bushes, fruit, twigs, tree bark, and roots. Elephants can grow up to weigh 13,000 pounds and 11 feet
high. Click here to observe elephants in their natural habitat!

Sushi!

There are hundreds of types of sushi in the world. To make sushi, chefs practice for 10 years to learn the knife
skills and precision of cutting fish. Sushi are mainly composed of white or brown rice amd cooked/raw seafood.
They are then served with soy sauce and wasabi. Go to this link for an awesome Dragon Roll recipe!
